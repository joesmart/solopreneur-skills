# Step 2.1: Brainstorm Candidate Solutions

**Apply the ReAct protocol from SKILL.md to execute this step.**

## Action

List at least 2-3 candidate solutions. For each:
- One-sentence summary of core approach
- Which files to create, which files to modify, what the key changes are

Do not evaluate yet — list all possibilities first.

## Checklist

Use these items in your **[CHECK]** stage:

- At least 3 candidate solutions generated
- Solutions are genuinely different in core approach (not just variations)
- At least 1 solution is "non-conventional" or creative
- Each solution specifies files to create/modify
- Have not started evaluation yet (pure brainstorming)

## Challenges

Use these questions in your **[CHALLENGE]** stage:

- Q1: What breaks this? → Are all my solutions based on same assumption? What if that assumption is wrong?
- Q2: What didn't I check? → Is there a solution I dismissed as "too complex" but might actually be optimal?
- Q3: Expert criticism? → If architect reviews my candidates, would they suggest a completely different approach?

## Minimum Loops

You must complete at least **2 loops** before you can PASS this step.
